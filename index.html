Instructions

1. Prepare your workbook. Ensure it contains all six required sheets with the original column names unchanged. Extra columns are ignored.

2. Upload. Drag the file onto the drop zone above, or click “Choose File”. Processing happens entirely in your browser — no data leaves your machine.

3. All tabs refresh automatically. Filters are reset to “All” and every chart, KPI card, and table updates to reflect the new data.

4. Hub & facility counts. The hub legend and facility filter are rebuilt from the new Facility_Testing sheet. If the workbook contains different hubs or facilities, the legend and dependent filter update accordingly.
